B For Boss

A Work In Progress


A first person shooter with parkour elements and big boss fights. Our team wants to try and combine the gameplay of Destiny raids, together with Shadow of Colossus bosses with a bit of a twist



Concept Art



Milestone 1


One of the main pillars for our game is parkour. We want to make sure the main mechanic feels and play well. To make sure we hit the sweet spot, we decided to only focus on the parkour aspect of the game as our milestone 1. To make things a bit more interesting, we also added a race course to test out different scenarios or even edge cases.



Milestone 2


For milestone 2, we wanted to focus on gun play together with parkour, since shooting is one of our main pillars. This would be considered the first iteration of our weapon system.
